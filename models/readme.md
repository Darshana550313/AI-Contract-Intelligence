## Categories

The CUAD dataset contains legal clause categories used for contract understanding and analysis. These categories represent important sections commonly found in legal agreements.

Examples include:

* Termination
* Confidentiality
* Payment Terms
* Assignment
* Governing Law
* Audit Rights
* Limitation of Liability
* Exclusivity
* License Grant

## Annotations

Annotations are labels attached to specific portions of contract text. Each annotation identifies the legal clause type associated with a particular section of a contract.

Example:

Contract Text:
"This agreement may be terminated by either party upon written notice."

Annotation:
Termination

These annotations help train NLP models to automatically identify and classify important legal clauses in contracts.
